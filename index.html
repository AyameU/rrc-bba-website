February News

We have had an excellent start to the new semester and are very happy with the progress made so far.

February is a very busy month with events happening throughout. There will be many opportunities for volunteer work, so follow the updates on our Facebook Page for early access to volunteer positions.

We are sending several our members to the Disrupted Conference on the 1st and 2nd to fulfill various roles.

The Honour Roll reception is on Monday the 12th.

The following week is where things really begin to pick up. Reading Week starts on the 19th. That week also kicks off the ACE department 50th anniversary dinner.

We are also helping with the Tech Fair the college has planned that week for the open house. We will be needing lots of help for that event.

Mid-Term Exams run the week of the 26th

Current Initiatives

We are currently working on an update to our website where we will be starting our own Student Tech Blog past, present, and future BBA members are all encouraged to submit content to our multi contributor blog. More information on this as we get closer to launch.

We are also working on a YouTube channel to provide some How To: videos that will cover topics that fall outside of the curriculum.

We hope to have these up and running by the end of the month.

The BBA is a team. As a team we have capabilities to accomplish things we could never do as individuals; be a part of something bigger, be apart of our team.
