Our Mission

Our mission is to build on the skills already taught to students and provide a sense of community to Red River College's Technology students by improving relationships between current students, instructors, industry and alumni. We accomplish this by providing volunteer opportunities, information/education sessions and social events.

Our Story

The Bits & Bytes Association (BBA) was created to improve communication between students and staff to enhance the quality of education for Introduction to Business Information Technology (IBIT), Business Information Technology (BIT) and Business Technology Management (BTM) programs offered at Red River College through the Applied Computer Education (ACE) department. Since its inception in 2015, BBA has grown steadily with over 100 members today and continutes to climb steadily.

How To Get Involved

Though involvement with the Bits & Bytes Association is encouraged by the ACE department, participation is not mandatory.
